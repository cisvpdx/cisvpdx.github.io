What is CISV?

CISV International was founded in 1950 by Dorris Allen and aims to educate and inspire action for a more just and peaceful world.

CISV operates in over 80 countries (National Associations) under the CISV international umbrella organization. Each National Associations supports its local chapters.

CISV Portland is currently establishing to be a recognized chapter.

What We Do

We are currently establishing ourselves as a registered non profit organization. The following activities are what we envision of CISV PDX:


Traveling

Whether it's going to a local or international camp, there will be opportunities for CISVers to get out of their comfort zones and travel.

Networking

CISV is a medium for its members to build tolerance and gain peace education with people from different backgrounds.

Volunteering

CISV members will learn the importance of giving back to the community through various outreach programs.
